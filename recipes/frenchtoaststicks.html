French Toast Sticks

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1/2 Cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 Cup milk
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp brownsugar
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 Slices white break cut into thirds
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a bowl mix eggs, powdered sugar, milk maple syrup, brown sugar, and cinnamon with a fork until well blended.
  3. Dip each bread stick into the egg mixture.
  4. Coat a skillet with nonstick spray and heat over medium heat. Place sticks in the hot skillet and cook until brown (2-3min).
  5. Turn over the sticks and cook the other side until brown (2-3min).
