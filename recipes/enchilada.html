Husband Never Leave Ya Enchilada

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow onion
  • 5 Small chicken breast
  • 1 Pound monterey jack cheese (Shredded)
  • 1 Big jalepeno pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon garlic
  • 1 Stick of butter
  • 1 Block of cream cheese
  • 1 Qt Heavy cream
  • 11 Small tortillas or 5 Large tortillas
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and paprika for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Put oil into a pan.
  3. Remove fat from chicken breast and cut into chunks.
  4. Place chicken chunks into oiled pan and season.
  5. While the chicken is cooking, chop up onion, jalapeno, and garlic.
  6. Once chicken is cooked, remove it from the pan, shred it, and place it into a seperate bowl. Make sure to leave the droppings in the pan.
  7. Place onion, jalapeno, and garlic into pan with butter.
  8. While waiting for that to cook down to your liking, shred your cheese and place it into a seperate bowl.
  9. Once the vegtables are cooked down, place cream cheese in the pan and stir until melted completely.
  10. Put shredded chicken into the cream cheese mixture and stir until mixed completely.
  11. Place mixture into torilla and roll it into a burrito. Place burritos into a casarole dish.
  12. Once all burritos have been placed in the pan, cover them with heavy whipping cream (not completely) and then top with shredded cheese.
  13. Place dish in the oven and cook for 30mins or until the cheese is slightly golden brown.
