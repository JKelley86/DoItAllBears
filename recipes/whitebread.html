Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 Cups lukewarm water (Can use 2 cups lukewarm milk and 4 cups luke warm water).
  • 2 Packages yeast
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 Cup oil or shortening
  • 4 Tablespoons salt
  • 8 Cups flour

Ingredients for cinnamon rolls

  • Butter (Softned).
  • white sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Put the water and yeast packets into large mixing bowl, let sit for a couple minutes until it looks milky or foamy.
  2. Add sugar, oil, salt, and flour into bowl. Mix and add flour stirring at first then knead when it gets to stiff. Knead until very stiff.
  3. Let rise in warm place. Punch down and let rise again.
  4. Punch down a second time and form into loaves.
  5. Let rise and bake at 325Â°F for 40 minutes.
  6. Produces 6 medium loaves.

Instructions for optional cinnamon rolls

  1. Take the dough that would make about 1 loaf and roll out.
  2. Spread heavy with soft butter.
  3. Sprinkle lightly with white sugar.
  4. Sprinkle heavily with brown sugar.
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon.
  6. Roll up and cut into 9 slices and put in well greased 9 X 9 pan.
  7. Bake 325 for 40 minutes or Until light brown. Tip out on alluminum foil.
