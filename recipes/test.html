Super Long Recipe Name That Might Get Cut Off

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and eggs together.
  3. Add the milk and vanilla extract, and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Allow to cool before serving.
