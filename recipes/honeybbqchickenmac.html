Honey BBQ Chicken Mac & Cheese

Recipe Details

Prep Time: N/A

Cook Time: N/A

Total Time: N/A

Ingredients (Cheese sauce)

  • 24 oz large elbow macaroni
  • 4 oz unsatled butter
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 12 oz can evaporated milk
  • 8 oz heavy cream
  • 8 oz milk
  • 8 oz pepper colby jack
  • 8 oz gouda
  • 8-12 oz sharp chedder (adjust based off thickness)
  • Salt to taste

Ingrediants (For layering))

  • 8 oz pepper colby jack
  • 8 oz sharp chedder

Ingredients (Chicken)

  • 2 Pounds chicken thighs cubed
  • 2 Cups buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1.5 Cups flour
  • 1 Cup panko

Ingredients (Honey Bbq Sauce)

  • 1/2 Cup ketchup
  • 1/4 Cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce of choice
  • 1/4 Tbsp honey
  • 3 Tbsp cold butter (Cut into small cubes)

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking your elbow macaroni noodles.
  2. Then shred your cheese.
  3. Then in a bowl combine cubed chicken, butter milk, hot sauce, and seasonings. Then mix and marinate.
  4. In a pan melt down butter add garlic powder, chili powder, and paprika. Let sit for a minute.
  5. Then add in evaporated milk then add heavy cream and wait until it starts to slightly bubble.
  6. Once it starts to lightly bubble add the shredded cheddard, colby jack, and gouda. Stir until melted and mix in elbow macaroni.
  7. Mix together the remaining colby jack and sharp cheddar.
  8. Layer macaroni and remaining cheese into a oven safe pan.
  9. Bake in oven for 20 mins at 350 Degrees
  10. While that is baking put flour, panko bread crumbs, garlic powder, chili powder, and paprika into a mixing bowl.
  11. Put chciken in dry mixture until coated and then fry in oil that is set to 350 Degrees for 5 minutes or until golden brown.
  12. Toss chicken in honey bbq sauce.
  13. Place chicken on mac and enjoy.
