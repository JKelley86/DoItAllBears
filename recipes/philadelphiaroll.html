Philadelphia Sushi Roll

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Piece seaweed (Or nori sheet)
  • 1/2 Cup sushi rice (Prepared)
  • 1 Avacado
  • 2oz Salmon or imitation crab meat (Optinal)
  • 1oz Cream cheese
  • Spicy mayo
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Put sushi rice on seaweed wrap and spread.
  2. Add cream cheese.
  3. Add avacado and salmon.
  4. Sprinkle sesame seeds.
  5. Roll ingredients together.
  6. Cut into slices.
  7. Top with spicy mayo.
