Pico De Gallo

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion finely chopped
  • 1 Medium jalapeno finely chopped
  • 1/4 Cup lime juice
  • 3/4 Teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1 1/2 Pounds ripe red tomatoes finely chopped (8 Small or 4 large)
  • 1/2 Cup finely chopped fresh cilantro (About 1 bunch)

Instructions

  1. In a medium serving bowl, combine the chopped onion, jalapeno, lime juice, and salt. Let marinate for about 5 minutes while you chop the tomatoes and cilantro.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and cilantro to the bowl and stir to combine. Taste, add more salt if the flavors dont quite sing.
  3. For the best flavor, let the mixture marinate for about 15 minutesor several hours in the fridge. Serve as a dip, or with a slotted spoon to avoid transferring to much tomato juice with your pico.
  4. Pico de gallo keeps well in the fridge, covered, for up to 4 days.
