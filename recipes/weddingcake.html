Wedding Cake

Recipe Details (Cake)

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Recipe Details (Buttercream Frosting)

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients (Cake)

  • 2 Boxes Betty Crocker yellow super moist cake mix
  • 6 Eggs
  • 2 Cups water
  • 1 1/2 Cup vegtiable oil

Ingredients (Buttercream Frosting)

  • 8 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 Cups powdered suger
  • 2/3 Cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/3 Cup milk
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions (Cake)

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F fo raluminum or glass pan or 325°F for nonstick pan. Grease pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix cake mix, water, oil, and eggs. Beat vigorously for 2 minutes and pour into pan.
  3. Bake as directed on box. Cool completely before frosting.

Instructions (Buttercream Frosting)

  1. In a large bowl beat butter until smooth. You can do this by hand or with mixer.
  2. Add powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and an optional pinch of salt. Beat them into the creamed butter (Use low speed if using an electric mixer then increase to medium speed).
  3. Add the vanilla to the milk then gradually beat in the mixture into the butter cream (Do this on low with electric mixer). Mix until smooth. If it's to stiff beat in more milk. If it's to thin beat in more powdered sugar until you reach the consistency you want.
  4. You can now use your buttercream to decorate your cake! If used on cake you can keep at cool room temperature for a few days.
  5. You can also store in a airtight container In the fridge for up to 2 weeks. Or freeze In an airtight container for up to 6 months.
