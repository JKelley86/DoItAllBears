Mac and Cheese

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound dried noodles (your choice)
  • 1 1/2 Cups of whlole or 2% milk
  • 2 TBSP flour
  • 2 to 3 Cups of shredded cheese (your choice)
  • Salt and Peper to your liking

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta, drain, and set aside.
  2. Place 1 cup of milk in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Mix the remaining milk in a seperate bowl with the flour and mix until no lumps.
  4. Pour remaining milk/flour mixture into the saucepan when the milk starts to steam. Stir vonstantly until the consistency is of heavy cream (3-4 mins).
  5. Once to heavy cream consistency, turn the heat to low and stir in cheese 1 cup at a time. Adjust how many cups of cheese and add milk if the sauce is too thick.
  6. Combine cheese sauce with noodles and enjoty.
