Nancy's Spaghetti Sauce

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 5-8 Hours

Total Time: 5.5-8.5 Hours

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound hamburger
  • 2 Pound Italian sausage
  • 2 Jars traditional prego sauce
  • 1 Jar ragu chunky sause with onion, tomatoe, and garlic
  • 1 Small onion
  • 1 Clove garlic
  • 4 Heeping tablespoons brown sugar
  • Red pepper
  • Parsley
  • Orgeno
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder

Instructions

  1. Cook hamburger with onion and garlic clove.
  2. Cook italian sausage.
  3. Mix everything in crockpot.
  4. Top with red pepper, parsley, oregeno, garlic powder, onion powder, and brown sugar.
  5. Cook 5 to 8 hours.
