Sprinklers

Here you will find any information on your sprinklers!

Zones

You have 9 zones. They are labled on the controller inside the garage. Each zone consist of 5 sprinklers. The shut off valve is in back by the AC and the solenoid valves are inbetween the shut off valve and the wood burried in the ground.

Zone 1: Front Sidewalk 10 min

Zone 2: Front Driveway 35 min

Zone 3: North Front/Back 30 min

Zone 4: North East Side 25 min

Zone 5: South Side 10 min

Zone 6: South East Corner 25 min

Zone 7: Drip Zone 10 min

Zone 8: Not Used

Zone 9: Garden 20 min

Winterization

1. Locate the shut off valve behind the house.

2. Bring air compressor next to shut off valve and start. Make sure to start it early to accumulate air.

3. Shut off the lower valve running into the house. This will shut off the water.

4. Start the first zone on the sprinkler controller by switching it to manual and selecting zone 1 then switch it to run.

5. Connect the air compressor to the air fitting inbetween the 2 valves. You will see zone 1 start to shoot out water.

6. Let the air compressor run until no more water shoots out. Ensure there is enough pressure.

7. Turn the top valve on and off as needed to accumulate enough air pressure in the air compressor.

8. Turn on the air to each zone at least twice to push out any remaning water that might have settled.

9. Repeat steps 5-8 for each zone ensure no water shoots out before moving on. This should take less than an hour.
