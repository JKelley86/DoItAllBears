Chapter One · Her Side

How We Met

My little sister may or may not have been snapchat crazy back then. I don't like adding random people on snapchat because that's just not what I do, or who I am. Meanwhile she was adding everyone on her quick add. She tried to get me to do the same, and I laughed at her repeatedly. I finally told her I would add one person only to make her happy and so she'd leave it alone. Adding that one person has led me to my best friend for life, Kaiden. Even though our relationship first began through a phone conversation it has grown to become so much more. What started as a boring snap streak quickly developed into meaningful and regular communication, allowing us to get to know one another on a deeper level. As we spent more time talking, we discovered shared values, interests, and a genuine connection that continued to grow stronger each day. After building a strong foundation through our conversations, we eventually met in person. Our bond became even stronger, and we realized that the connection we had formed over the phone was real and meaningful. Since then, we have continued to nurture our relationship through mutual love, trust, support, and commitment. Over time, our relationship has grown into a loving partnership, and we have shared many memorable experiences together. We have supported one another through life's challenges and celebrated its joys side by side. Our love and dedication to each other have only deepened, and we are now excited to take the next step in our journey together as we prepare for marriage and a lifetime of happiness together.

Chapter Two

The First Date

Our first official date was on December 13th, at the Mall of America. Looking back, it's funny how nervous we both were. The night before was actually the first time we had ever met in person, and I was incredibly quiet and shy. Kaiden kept trying to get me to talk, but I was so nervous that I had no idea how to start or keep a conversation going. The next day, we spent the entire day together at the Mall of America. We arrived when it opened and stayed until almost closing time. It was a long day, but it was such a good one. We walked all over the mall, shopped, talked, and got to know each other better as the day went on. At one point while we were walking around, I finally stopped and asked Kaiden what we were. He had never actually said anything beforehand, and I honestly didn't know where we stood. He looked at me and said that he thought it was obvious and that he had assumed we were already together. I told him that I didn't know because he had never actually asked me. He then asked if he really needed to say it out loud, and I giggled as I nodded yes. That's when he finally asked me to be his girlfriend. After that, the rest of the day felt even more special. We continued shopping, laughing, and enjoying our time together, knowing exactly where we stood. It became a memory that I will always cherish because it marked the beginning of our relationship. Later that week, we went on our second date to Olive Garden, where we enjoyed a date night just the two of us and continued building the connection that had started at the mall.

Chapter Three

Through the Years

On January 13th, I moved in with Kaiden after having surgery that same morning. It was the start of a new chapter for us, and since then, we have continued to grow together in so many ways. Through every challenge, milestone, and memory, we have built a life that feels truly ours. One of the biggest moments in our journey came when I surprised Kaiden with our fur baby, Remi, on February 8, 2025. Back then, she was just a tiny little thing, and now she has grown into our lovable little menace. Originally, my "cousin" Paige was supposed to bring Remi to us one weekend, but she got extremely sick and couldn't make the trip. Kaiden and I were both so disappointed because we had been so excited to welcome our puppy home. Since Kaiden had drill that weekend, I went down to my mom's house to spend time with family. What Kaiden didn't know was that my mom and I were about to make a ten-hour drive down to Indiana to pick up Remi ourselves. I turned off all of my locations, which immediately made him suspicious. He kept asking what I was doing and why he couldn't see where I was. I felt bad for keeping the secret and making up excuses, but I wanted the surprise to be perfect. We left early Saturday morning and drove ten hours to Indiana. After picking up Remi, we made the long trip back and arrived home on Sunday morning, February 8th. Later that same day, Kaiden was finishing drill and heading home. I managed to beat him there, and with the help of our roommates, we got everything set up for the surprise. Kaiden had told me earlier that day that the drill hadn't gone the way he wanted and that he was having a rough day. Even though he was in a grumpy mood, everything changed the moment he saw Remi. The look in his eyes and the smile on his face made every mile of that road trip worth it. It was one of those moments I'll never forget. Ever since Remi came into our lives, our little family has only grown stronger. She has become such a huge part of our lives and our relationship. One of her favorite things in the world is going on car rides. Because we live far away from many of our family members and friends, we often take road trips to visit everyone, and Remi comes along every chance she gets. Over the years, we've traveled from Lakeville to Fairmont and now from Anoka to Fairmont. While being far away from loved ones isn't always easy, those trips give us time together and allow Remi to visit all of her favorite people and furry friends. The hardest part is always leaving because she never wants to go home. As much as she loves visiting my parents in Fairmont and spending time with her friends there, I think she might love visiting Kaiden's parents in Rush City even more. She runs endlessly around the yard, never wanting to come inside. Once she does come in, she spends her time bothering the other dogs, even when they clearly tell her to leave them alone. She's a lot like her father in that way—neither of them listen very well, and it always gives me something to laugh about. Over the last year, Kaiden and I have had our tough times and our easier times, but through it all, we have continued to support one another. We push each other to be better, encourage each other when things get difficult, and celebrate the good moments together. Today, we live in our own place with no roommates, and we are grateful for it every day. Some of my favorite memories are the simple ones—cooking together in the kitchen and accidentally dropping food for Remi (though we all know it's not really an accident), or spending a lazy day on the couch doing absolutely nothing except scrolling on our phones and enjoying each other's company. Life hasn't always been perfect, but every year has brought us closer together. Through every road trip, every challenge, every laugh, and every quiet moment at home, we've continued to build a life filled with love, happiness, and memories that will last forever.
