Workbench Build

A sturdy and reliable workbench perfect for any woodworking shop.

Project Overview

This workbench is designed to provide a stable and spacious surface for all your woodworking needs. Built with heavy-duty lumber and reinforced joints, it can handle large projects and heavy tools with ease.

Bill of Materials (BOM)

  • 4x4 Pressure-Treated Posts - 4 pieces (Legs)
  • 2x4 Lumber - 8 pieces (Frame)
  • 3/4" Plywood Sheet - 1 piece (Top)
  • Wood Screws (3") - 50 pieces
  • Wood Glue - 1 bottle
  • Wood Stain or Finish - 1 can

Cut List

  • 4x4 Posts - 4 pieces @ 32" (Legs)
  • 2x4 Lumber - 4 pieces @ 48" (Long Frame Sides)
  • 2x4 Lumber - 4 pieces @ 20" (Short Frame Sides)
  • 3/4" Plywood Sheet - 1 piece @ 48" x 24" (Top Surface)

Assembly Steps

  1. Cut all pieces according to the cut list.
  2. Assemble the frame using 2x4s and wood screws, forming a rectangle.
  3. Attach the 4x4 posts as legs at each corner of the frame.
  4. Secure the plywood top onto the frame using wood glue and screws.
  5. Sand the entire workbench and apply wood stain or finish for durability.
