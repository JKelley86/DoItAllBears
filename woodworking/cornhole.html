Regulation Cornhole Boards Build

Build your own regulation cornhole boards for the perfect backyard game setup.

Project Overview

These regulation cornhole boards are built to official specifications, providing a stable, smooth surface for the perfect game experience. Crafted with high-quality materials and detailed instructions, these boards will last for many years of enjoyment.

Bill of Materials (BOM)

  • 2x4 Lumber - 6 pieces (Frame)
  • 1/2" Plywood Sheet - 2 pieces (Top Surface)
  • Wood Screws (2") - 40 pieces
  • Wood Glue - 1 bottle
  • Wood Stain or Finish - 1 can
  • Handles (optional) - 2 pieces

Cut List

  • 2x4 Lumber - 4 pieces @ 48" (Long Frame Sides)
  • 2x4 Lumber - 4 pieces @ 24" (Short Frame Sides)
  • 1/2" Plywood Sheet - 2 pieces @ 48" x 24" (Top Surface)

Assembly Steps

  1. Cut all pieces according to the cut list.
  2. Assemble the frame using 2x4s and wood screws, forming a rectangle.
  3. Attach the 1/2" plywood top onto the frame using wood glue and screws.
  4. If desired, attach handles to the side of the board for easy portability.
  5. Sand the entire cornhole board and apply wood stain or finish for durability.
