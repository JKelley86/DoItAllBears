Hunting Seasons & Zones

Deer Hunting

  • Archery: Mid-September to Late December
  • Firearm: Early November to Mid-November (Varies by Zone)
  • Muzzleloader: Late November to Mid-December

Zones: 100 Series (Northeast), 200 Series (Central), 300 Series (Southeast)

Waterfowl Hunting

  • North Zone: Late September to Late December
  • Central Zone: Late September, then October to December
  • South Zone: Split season structure

Upland Bird Hunting

  • Grouse: Mid-September to Early January
  • Pheasant: Mid-October to Early January

Fishing Seasons & Zones

General Fishing Opener

Two weeks before Memorial Day (May 10-11 in 2024)

Species-Specific Seasons

  • Walleye, Sauger, Pike: Opener to Late February
  • Muskie: Starts Early June
  • Stream Trout: Opens Mid-April
  • Lake Trout: Mid-May to Late September

Data sourced from the Minnesota Department of Natural Resources
